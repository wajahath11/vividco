MerchMind Planning Tool

Context.

CPG companies need effective assortment planning to optimize their product offerings and ensure they meet consumer demands and preferences. The right product mix can boost revenue and minimize resource waste. However, many CPG companies still depend on outdated methods like spreadsheets and disconnected tools, which hinder collaboration and lead to inefficiencies. By embracing advanced, integrated assortment planning solutions, businesses can better align their inventory with market trends, enhance decision-making, and drive greater profitability.
